Welcome to the G36 Improvement Project! The improvement project started as a simple edit of some configuration files but it has since grown into a fully-fledged modification that improves all aspects of the default G36 and introduces new features. FrettFS brought together the original team (including myself) which consisted of both flight simulator enthusiasts and real G36 pilots. As often happens the original team got smaller as real life committments built and the mod is now maintained just by me with help from a dedicated testing team of real pilots on our Discord.

This panel is very much a work in progress, there will definately be things missing or buggy until I get it to a more stable place. My plan is for this window to mimic the operations in a similar nature to that of an A2A aircraft. Ultimately, there will be a preflight section that you will use to check your aircraft is airworthy prior to flight, a maintenance hangar to fix issues with the aircraft, a place to monitor the mod status, and an about page with the latest change log etc. Also planned are an interactive weight/balance chart and performance charts for use by pilots in flight.

This is very much a community project so please do get involved, give feedback and chat about what you'd like to see. You can join our community in the MSFS forum https://forums.flightsimulator.com/t/g36-improvement-project/ or on our discord server (search for 'Simulated.Flights') https://discord.gg/JVPMYdKx86

Thanks for downloading our Mod!
